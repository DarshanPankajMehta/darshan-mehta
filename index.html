About me
Engineer by training,
problem-solver by nature

I'm a Senior Software Engineer at PTC Software India, where I architect and build AI-driven systems — most recently a RAG-based Log Analyzer that automates root-cause analysis for enterprise PLM platforms.

Before PTC, I spent over 3 years at Mercedes-Benz R&D India building ML inference pipelines and sensor data systems for Smart Door technology — improving intent classification accuracy from 64% to 89% using Kalman filters and LSTM models.

I hold an M.Tech in Data Analytics from NIT Trichy and a B.Tech in Computer Science from Medi-Caps University. I've been recognized with Silver, Bronze, and Innovation awards at Mercedes-Benz for AI-enabled system contributions.

Outside of work, I build side projects (like GeoScope — a real-time geopolitics AI dashboard), stay active in the open-source community, and mentor students through Rang De Zindagi NGO.
